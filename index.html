About Me

I am a passionate and forward-thinking Computer Science student with a strong inclination toward technology's transformative power. My journey in tech began with experimenting on simple HTML websites—a hands-on curiosity that soon grew into a deep fascination with how digital systems operate, interact, and secure the data and experiences they support.

Over the years, this passion has evolved into a solid foundation in software development, system architecture, and cybersecurity. I've explored front-end and back-end development, delved into network protocols, and designed secure systems that reflect a balance between usability and protection. I believe that in our ever-connected world, secure systems are no longer optional—they're essential.

My interests span across:

  • Cybersecurity – from penetration testing to ethical hacking and digital forensics.
  • System Architecture – creating scalable, modular solutions for real-world problems.
  • AI & Automation – exploring how intelligent systems can optimize workflows and improve decision-making.
  • Web & App Development – building responsive, intuitive platforms with a focus on user experience.

Every line of code I write and every system I design is rooted in a desire to make tech smarter, safer, and more inclusive. Whether I'm collaborating in a team or tackling solo projects, I bring a mindset that's curious, analytical, and purpose-driven.

"Secure systems, secure futures."

This is not just a motto—it's a mission. A reminder that innovation must go hand-in-hand with responsibility.
